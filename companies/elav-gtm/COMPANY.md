ELAV GTM is a fully operational AI company that runs your Go-To-Market from Telegram, Slack, or any messaging channel. Each agent is a specialist with its own memory, tools, and expertise. The orchestrator delegates tasks to the right agent, coordinates parallel execution, and synthesizes results.

**The org chart:**

1. **Atlas** (Orchestrator) — routes tasks, delegates to specialists, synthesizes results
2. **Radar Hayden** — prospect research, lead generation, outbound targeting
3. **Scout Magnus** — deep research, technical intelligence, competitive analysis
4. **Vector Sterling** — account nurture, relationship follow-up, personalized outreach
5. **Tower Kane** — pipeline management, deal tracking, CRM operations
6. **Beacon Cross** — market analysis, competitor monitoring, industry trends
7. **Charter Reid** — compliance, regulatory checks, audit documentation
8. **Bridge Falcon** — technical implementation, demos, engineering support

The philosophy: specialists beat generalists. Each agent does one thing exceptionally well. The orchestrator knows who to call for what. Shared memory means the whole team learns from every conversation, every deal, every market signal.

Running on CodesDevs OÜ operations since January 2026. Battle-tested on real pipeline, real deals, real revenue.
