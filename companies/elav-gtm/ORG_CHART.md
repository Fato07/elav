## Delegation Flow

### Strategic Issues (Top-Down)
1. **Atlas** creates strategic initiatives
2. **C-Suite** breaks down into departmental objectives
3. **Department heads** execute with their specialized skills
4. **Results** flow back up: Department → C-Suite → Atlas

### Cross-Departmental Coordination
- All cross-department requests flow through **Atlas**
- **Coeus (COO)** facilitates operational coordination
- C-Suite members collaborate directly for their domains

### Escalation Rules
- **30-minute rule:** If stuck >30min, escalate to manager
- **Scope boundary:** If decision exceeds role scope, escalate
- **Resource conflicts:** Escalate to Atlas through appropriate C-Suite leader

## Communication Rhythm
- **Daily:** Departmental operations, progress updates
- **Weekly:** C-Suite coordination, strategic alignment
- **Monthly:** Cross-departmental planning, performance review
- **Quarterly:** Strategic planning, organizational optimization
